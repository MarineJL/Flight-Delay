Warning of possible flight delay

Based on data extracted from Bureau of Transportation Statistics for 2016, here is a tool to estimate your flight delay (or, let's be foolish, early arrival!). To do so, you are asked to provide the following information:

  • Date of departure (format dd/mm/yyyy)
  • Departure hour (format hhmm)
  • Arrival hour (format hhmm)
  • Scheduled flight time (in min)
  • Departure Airport
  • Destination Airport
  • Airline company
